About Us

KAASH - LIGHT ENGINEERS

A professional lighting consultancy for all things light and bright. Our human-centric lighting designs reflect our commitment to functionality as well as sustainability and are thoughtfully executed to help you create your dream space.

We aim to combine modern technology with energy efficient lighting schemes in the best interest of our clients as well as the planet.

Services we provide

Light + Sustainability + Technology

Human-Centric Lighting

Working with light for more than a decade, we are of strong opinion that the concept of HCL goes beyond technology into holistic light planning. Light is for people, their task and its environment, which is where the idea of human centric lighting begins - with people at it core.

Sustainability

Reducing LIGHT CARBON of cities and buildings with our sustainable solutions to create a healthier planet and its ecosystem.Sustainable light planning provides a greenfield opportunity to contribute towards UN sustainable development goals and EU green deal.

Technology

Light is a unifying science with power to improve our lives. Evolving technologies, now make it posisble to manipulate light and procreate any desired environment to support space function or to build an experience. Power of technology provides metaverse of opportunities.
